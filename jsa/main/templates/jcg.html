Japanese Conversation Group

Whether you're a total beginner learning your first Hiragana or a native speaker looking for community, JCG is your weekly space to practice and connect.

What happens at JCG?

JCG isn't a formal class—it's a relaxed hangout. We break into small groups based on skill level. Some days we play language games, other days we just chat about hobbies, anime, or life in Ottawa over snacks.

Beginner Friendly

Practice basic greetings and survival phrases.

Advanced Circles

Deep dives into culture and natural slang.

No Sign-ups Required

Just show up! JCG is free for all uOttawa students. You don't need to be a JSA member to drop in and see what it's like.
